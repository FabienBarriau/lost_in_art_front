Welcome in WikiArt Viz, this app propose an original way to explore the collection of WikiArt website. The goal is to vizualize multiples artworks in the same big picture where each one are place in a coherent area, those that contains the same object/content or that have a similar color are regrouped in the same area.

  • Choose categories of artwork that you want to see. (Media, Style, Artiste, Genre)
  • Choose how pictures will be regrouped: by content or by color. (Metric)
  • Click on the Validate button, it will launch the construction of the big picture
  • When the big picture is loaded you can move on the graph thanks to your mouse, press and hold right to move, left to rotate and scroll to zoom.
  • You can also add details about an image in this panel by right-clicking on it, to deselect this one re-click on it.

Warning: It can take several minutes to load the big picture of all artworks. Try to apply some filters to familiarise with the app
